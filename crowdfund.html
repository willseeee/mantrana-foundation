Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about contributing to MANTRANA FOUNDATION campaigns.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! MANTRANA FOUNDATION is eligible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. You will receive an official receipt for your contribution that you can use to claim tax deductions when filing your returns.

How do I know my money reaches the right people?

We publish quarterly transparency reports detailing exactly how funds are utilized. You also receive campaign-specific impact updates via email. 100% of your donation goes directly to beneficiaries — MANTRANA FOUNDATION covers all administrative costs separately.

Can I donate to a specific campaign?

Absolutely. Each campaign card has a dedicated "Donate Now" button that pre-selects that campaign. You can also choose a specific campaign from the dropdown in the custom donation form. If you'd like your contribution to go wherever it's needed most, select "Any / General Fund".

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit Cards, and Wallets. All transactions are SSL-encrypted for your security. Receipts are automatically emailed within 24 hours of a successful payment.

Can my company or organization run a corporate campaign?

Yes! We welcome CSR partnerships. Reach out via our Contact page or email mantranafoundation@gmail.com to discuss a customized corporate giving campaign that aligns with your company's social responsibility goals.
