Our Background

MANTRANA FOUNDATION was established in 2025 with the objective of promoting entrepreneurship and self-reliance in society. As a non-governmental organization (NGO), we believe in connecting those people and groups to the mainstream who are left behind due to lack of resources or information.

We are dedicated to bridging the gap between potential and opportunity, ensuring that every aspiring entrepreneur has the guidance and support they need to succeed.

Our Impact & Strategy

The main goal of the foundation is to spread awareness about various government schemes such as Startup India, Digital MSME, and other SHG programs. We ensure that an effective marketing system is developed for the products of small entrepreneurs and groups.

Through both online and offline platforms, we provide product promotion, branding, exhibitions, and modern digital marketing to connect small businesses directly with national and global markets.

Our Vision & Resolve

"We resolve to build a strong and self-reliant nation based on indigenous values—where our language, traditional attire, and local businesses become the basis of our identity and pride. So that India establishes itself as a leading nation with its unique identity on the global stage."
